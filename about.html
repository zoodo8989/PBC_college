About

Panskura Banamali College (Autonomus)


Panskura Banamali College is a renowned educational institution in West Bengal that offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs:


Courses


The college offers courses in a wide range of subjects, including science, humanities, commerce, teaching and education, arts, IT and software, and medicine and health sciences. Some of the most popular programs include B.A., B.Com, B.Ed, B.P. Ed, B.Sc., BCA, M.A., M.P. Ed, and M.Sc.


Infrastructure


The college has a modern infrastructure that includes a library with an OPAC system, internet facilities, and filtered drinking water.


Faculti


The college has a group of experienced and professional faculty members.


History


The college was established in 1960 by Late Banamali Charan Khatua, a wealthy and cultured person of Panskura. The land for the college was donated by Late Hasmat Ali Khan and his co-sharers.


Affiliation


The college was affiliated with the University of Calcutta until 1984, when it was affiliated with Vidyasagar University. In 2018-19, the University Grants Commission (UGC) granted the college autonomous status.


Recognition


The college has been recognized as a research center in the faculty of science and has received grants from the Government of India and the Government of West Bengal.
